Creating courses

Step 1: Plan your course structure

Before creating your course, outline the learning objectives, lessons, and assessments. This helps ensure a clear learning path.

  • Define your learning objectives
  • Break content into manageable lessons
  • Plan assessments and quizzes
  • Consider prerequisite knowledge

Step 2: Add course content

Use the course editor to add your content. You can include text, videos, images, and interactive elements.

  • Write clear, engaging lesson content
  • Add multimedia resources where helpful
  • Include examples and practice exercises
  • Organize content in a logical sequence

Step 3: Publish and share

Once your course is ready, publish it and share it with learners. You can set access permissions and enrollment options.
